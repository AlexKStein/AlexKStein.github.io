About Me

I am currently a student at We Can Code IT, and the projects below were created during my time there. In time, as I continue to work on newer and more exciting projects, and learning everything I can along the way, so will my projects section from these new and exciting endeavors.

Through a life full of hard work and a love of learning, I've learned a great many things; most importantly is that I basically know nothing in the grand scheme of things.
